# Note 78: Compliance Considerations

## Summary
Operating a game with crypto rewards intersects with regulations. Document baseline compliance work.

## Implementation Notes
- Identify jurisdictions involved and consult legal counsel regarding gaming, gambling, and securities laws.
- Implement KYC/AML checks proportionate to prize values; integrate third-party providers if necessary.
- Draft clear Terms of Service and Privacy Policy referencing data handling, cookies, and blockchain interactions.
- Store consent logs for marketing emails and analytics tracking.

## Observability
- Track completion rates for required compliance steps (KYC pass/fail) to spot drop-offs.
- Monitor legal/regulatory changes relevant to Celo or gaming.

## Next Steps
- Document breach notification procedures and data retention policies.
- Schedule periodic compliance reviews alongside security assessments.
